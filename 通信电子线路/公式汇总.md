### 谐振频率

- **空载**
  $$
  f_0=\frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{LC}}
  $$

- **有载**
  $$
  f=\frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{L_{\sum}C_{\sum}}}
  $$

### 品质因数

- **空载**
  $$
  Q_0=\frac{1}{g_{e0}\omega_0L}=\frac{\omega_0C}{g_{e0}}=\frac{1}{g_{e0}}\sqrt{\frac{C}{L}}
  $$

- **有载**
  $$
  Q_e=\frac{1}{g_{\sum}\omega L_{\sum}}=\frac{\omega C_{\sum}}{g_{\sum}}=\frac{1}{g_{\sum}}\sqrt{\frac{C_{\sum}}{L_{\sum}}}
  $$
  此处的 $\omega$ 与有载的谐振频率关联。

### 谐振电导

$$
g_{e0}=\frac{1}{Q_0\omega_0L}=\frac{\omega_0C}{Q_0}=\frac{1}{Q_0}\sqrt{\frac{C}{L}}
$$

### 通频带

$$
BW_{0.7}=\frac{f}{Q_e}
$$

### 阻抗变换

$$
R_L'=\frac{1}{n^2}R_L,\ g_L'=n^2g_L
$$

### 电压增益

$$
\begin{aligned}
\dot{A}_u&=\frac{n_1n_2y_{fe}}{g_{e0}+n_1^2g_{oe}+n_2^2g_{ie}} \\\\
C_{\sum}&=C+n_1^2C_{oe}+n_2^2C_{ie}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 多级通频带

$$
B_{0.7(n)}=B_{0.7(1)}\sqrt{2^{\frac{1}{n}}-1}
$$

### 功率

$$
\begin{aligned}
P_D&=U_{CC}I_{c0} \\\\
P_O&=\frac{1}{2}U_{cm}I_{c1m}=\frac{1}{2}I_{c1m}^2R_{\sum} \\\\
P_C&=P_D-P_O \\\\
\eta_C&=\frac{P_O}{P_D}=\frac{1}{2}\frac{U_{cm}}{U_{CC}}\frac{I_{c1m}}{I_{c0}}=\frac{1}{2}\xi g_1(\theta)
\end{aligned}
$$

> 参数补充

$$
\begin{aligned}
&I_{c1m}=I_{cm}\alpha_1(\theta),\ I_{c0}=I_{cm}\alpha_0(\theta)\dots \\\\
&U_{cm}=I_{c1m}R_{\sum} \\\\
&\xi=\frac{U_{cm}}{U_{CC}} \\\\
&g_1(\theta)=\frac{\alpha_1(\theta)}{\alpha_0(\theta)}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 折线近似分析法

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\cos\theta=\frac{U_{on}-U_{BB}}{U_{bm}} \\\\
&I_{cm}=gU_{bm}(1-\cos\theta) \\\\
&g=\frac{I_{cm}}{U_{bm}(1-\cos\theta)}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 图解法

$$
\begin{aligned}
&g_{cr}=\frac{I_{cm}}{U_{CC}-U_{cm}} \\\\
&g_d=\frac{I_{cm}}{U_{cm}(1-\cos\theta)}=g\frac{U_{bm}}{U_{cm}} \\\\
&R_d=\frac{1}{g_d}=\alpha_1(\theta)R_{\sum}(1-\cos\theta)
\end{aligned}
$$

### 载波信号

$$
u_C(t)=U_{cm}\cos\omega_ct
$$

### 调制信号

$$
u_{\Omega}(t)=U_{\Omega m}\cos\Omega t
$$

### 振幅

$$
U_{max}=U_{cm}(1+m_a) \\\\
U_{min}=U_{cm}(1-m_a)
$$

### 载波功率

$$
P_C=\frac{1}{2}\frac{U_{cm}^2}{R_L}=\frac{P_{AM}}{1+\frac{1}{2}m_a^2}
$$

### 边频功率

$$
2P_{SB}=\frac{1}{2}m_a^2P_C
$$

### 总输出功率

$$
P_{AM}=P_C+2P_{SB}=\left(1+\frac{1}{2}m_a^2\right)P_C
$$

### 积化和差

$$
\cos\alpha\cos\beta=\frac{1}{2}[\cos(\alpha+\beta)+\cos(\alpha-\beta)]
$$

### AM 调幅波

$$
\begin{aligned}
u_{AM}(t)&=U_{cm}(1+m_a\cos\Omega t)\cos\omega_ct \\
&=U_{cm}\left[\cos\omega_ct+\frac{1}{2}m_a\cos(\omega_c+\Omega)t+\frac{1}{2}m_a\cos(\omega_c-\Omega)t\right]
\end{aligned}
$$

### 双边带调幅波

$$
\begin{aligned}
u_{DSB}(t)&=ku_{\Omega}(t)u_C(t) \\
&=kU_{\Omega m}\cos\Omega tU_{cm}\cos\omega_ct \\
&=\frac{1}{2}kU_{\Omega m}U_{cm}[\cos(\omega_c+\Omega)t+\cos(\omega_c-\Omega)t] \\\\
U_m&=\frac{1}{2}kU_{\Omega m}U_{cm} \\
P_{AM}&=2P_{SB}=2\cdot\frac{U_m^2}{R_L}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 带宽

$$
BW=2f_{\Omega}=\frac{\Omega}{\pi}
$$

### 总输入功率

$$
P_D=U_{CC}I_{c0},\ P_{\Omega}=\frac{1}{2}m_a^2P_D \\
\Rightarrow P_{in}=P_D+P_{\Omega}=\left(1+\frac{1}{2}m_a^2\right)P_D
$$

### 集电极效率

$$
\eta_C=\frac{P_{AM}}{P_{in}}
$$

### FM 调频信号

$$
\begin{aligned}
&u_{FM}(t)=U_{cm}\cos(\omega_ct+m_f\sin\Omega t) \\\\
&\Delta\omega_m=k_fU_{\Omega m}=m_f\Omega \\\\
&m_f=\frac{\Delta\omega_m}{\Omega}=\frac{k_fU_{\Omega m}}{\Omega}
\end{aligned}
$$

### PM 调相信号

$$
\begin{aligned}
&u_{PM}(t)=U_{cm}\cos(\omega_ct+m_p\cos\Omega t) \\\\
&\Delta\omega_m=k_pU_{\Omega m}\Omega=m_p\Omega \\\\
&m_p=\frac{\Delta m}{\Omega}=\frac{k_pU_{\Omega m}\Omega}{\Omega}=k_pU_{\Omega m}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 最大相偏与通频带

$$
M=\frac{\Delta f_m}{F},\ BW=2(\Delta f_m+F_{max})
$$
